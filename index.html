Jonathan "JT" Wu | Strategic Advisor & Civic Leader

Jonathan "JT" Wu is a seasoned strategic finance advisor and civic entrepreneur with deep expertise in mergers & acquisitions, corporate strategy, and cross-sector leadership. As Chairman of Georgia's largest public library system and founder of nationally recognized organizations, JT brings a distinguished blend of private-sector acumen and public service to every engagement.

Beyond the private sector, JT is a recognized leader in public-private partnerships and civic innovation. He serves as Chairman of the Gwinnett County Public Library System, where he has delivered transformational growth and operational excellence. He is also the Founder and Executive Director of Preface, a nationally recognized early childhood literacy nonprofit, and previously liaised with the U.S. State Department on diplomatic and international development initiatives.

A sought-after speaker and thought leader, JT has been featured in national publications and recognized as one of Georgia's most influential civic leaders. He received his A.B. cum laude in Public and International Affairs from Princeton University and maintains active involvement in professional and philanthropic organizations across the Southeast.

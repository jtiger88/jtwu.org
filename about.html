Jonathan "JT" Wu

Strategic Finance Advisor & Civic Leader

Jonathan "JT" Wu is a seasoned strategic finance advisor and civic entrepreneur with deep expertise in mergers & acquisitions, corporate strategy, and cross-sector leadership. As Chairman of Georgia's largest public library system and founder of nationally recognized organizations, JT brings a distinguished blend of private-sector acumen and public service to every engagement.

Through his advisory firm Magnolia Prospect, JT provides white-glove strategic finance services to business owners, family offices, and civic institutions. His expertise spans sell-side M&A execution, capital raises, fractional CFO services, and complex transaction structuring. He has successfully guided clients through hundreds of millions in transaction value, combining sophisticated financial modeling with stakeholder communication excellence.

Beyond the private sector, JT is a recognized leader in public-private partnerships and civic innovation. He serves as Chairman of the Gwinnett County Public Library System, where he has delivered transformational growth and a 10x return on public investment. He is also the Founder and Executive Director of Preface, a nationally recognized early childhood literacy nonprofit that has trained thousands of high school ambassadors to mentor young students through a proprietary multilingual platform.

JT's civic leadership extends to board positions with the Gwinnett Chamber of Commerce and previous liaison work with the U.S. State Department on international development initiatives. He has provided testimony on education innovation and economic development, and previously founded Emerging Voters, a nonpartisan voter engagement organization focused on diverse communities.

A sought-after speaker and thought leader, JT has been featured in national publications and recognized as one of Georgia's most influential civic leaders. He received his A.B. cum laude in Public and International Affairs from Princeton University, where he was a varsity coxswain on the heavyweight rowing team. He maintains active involvement in professional and philanthropic organizations across the Southeast.
